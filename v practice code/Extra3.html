What is Cricket All About?


  • It is a Bat and ball game played between two teams of 11 players each.
  • The game is played in a field with a 22 yard long pitch in the center of the field.
  • Cricket Ball, Cricket Bat, Stumps, Bails are some of the equipment's used in Cricket.
  • The game is played in three different formats:
    • Test Match: A 5 days game.
    • One Day: Limited 50 over game for each side
    • T20: Limited 20 over game for each side
    • International Cricket Council-ICC, is the highest level Cricket governing body.

Interesting facts about Cricket


Here are some interesting facts about Cricket:


  • Considered as world's 2nd most popular sport.
  • The Game is said to be originated in England.
  • The first recorded game was played in 1646.
  • Cricket is still not a part of Olympics.
  • The game ismost popular in Australia, England, Indian Sub Continent, the West Indies and Douthern Africa.
