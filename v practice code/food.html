Citrus Junction

All-day Fine Dining Restaurant in Gandhinagar

Citrus Junction as the name suggests is inspired by the age-old Indian railways. The décor and architecture of the restaurant capture the pivotal role railways played in the lives of Indian people over the last century. The restaurant has been conceptualized around the odyssey of luxury trains of yesteryear with their elaborate dining cars, serviced by the very best of chefs and personal butlers.

The interior decor of the restaurant is reminiscent of the old carriageways, private cabins, exposed ceilings, Burma teak doors, and exclusive use of brass. The artwork leading to the brassiere announces our disembarkation at this awe-inspiring culinary juncture.

Citrus Junction is an all-day casual dining restaurant in Gandhinagar with interactive world cuisines including Japanese, Oriental, Indian, Continental, Italian, and Middle Eastern. You can walk up to our live cooking stations where our chefs will curate dishes to suit your palate.

Led by a team of culinary artists, using the freshest and the finest authentic ingredients, our focus is on healthy dining. Most of our food is cooked à la minute. Chafing dishes are select, encouraging small quantities of advanced cooking on the buffet. Oven fresh breakfast rolls, breads, pizzas, and exclusively curated bakes will charm your inner epicurean.
